Bilbo Baggins

Burglar - Ring-Bearer - Elf-Friend

A lone hobbit that lives in the shire. He is recruited by Gandalf the Gray and a small band of Dwarves to embark on a great adventure! On his journey, Bilbo happens to find a mysterious ring in an underground bog. The ring helps him escape from Gollem, a creature molded from the dark and the corruption of the ring.
